Day-Zero is a situation which arises when the groundwater of a region runs out and the officials are forced to turn off the water supply in the city. The Indian city of Bengaluru being designated as the city of Gardens is also following the footsteps of Cape Town and is going
to experience Day-Zero momentarily. According to a report by Niti Aayog, a think-tank
department of Indian government for sustainable development around the country,
Bangalore as well as 20 other major cities of the country including Delhi, Hyderabad etc. are expected to reach zero groundwater level, affecting the lives of 100 million residents.
